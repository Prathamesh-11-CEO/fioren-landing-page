Shipping & Delivery Policy

Effective Date: 2026Last Updated: 2026

This Shipping & Delivery Policy (“Policy”) governs all purchases made from Fioren, operated by Bioreach Pharma Exports Pvt. Ltd., through our official website, Amazon storefront, marketplaces, and authorized sales channels.

By placing an order with Fioren, customers agree to the terms outlined below.

Order Processing

All orders are subject to:

  • Product availability
  • Payment verification
  • Fraud prevention screening
  • Order confirmation

Orders are generally processed within 1–3 business days, excluding Sundays, public holidays, and unforeseen operational disruptions.

Fioren reserves the right to delay, hold, or cancel any order if:

  • Payment authorization fails
  • Fraudulent activity is suspected
  • Incorrect pricing or inventory issues occur
  • Shipping restrictions apply

Prepaid Orders Only (No Cash on Delivery)

Fioren operates on a 100% prepaid payment model. We currently do NOT offer Cash on Delivery (COD), Pay on Delivery, or Partial COD services. Customers must complete payment through approved online payment methods before order dispatch.

Accepted payment methods may include:

  • UPI
  • Debit/Credit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Wallets
  • Razorpay-supported payment methods

Orders will only be processed after successful payment confirmation.

Shipping Partners

Orders may be shipped through trusted third-party logistics providers including:

  • Shiprocket
  • National courier partners
  • Regional delivery partners

Courier partner allocation is determined solely by Fioren based on serviceability, delivery efficiency, operational feasibility, and location coverage.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship across selected serviceable locations within India. International shipping availability, if applicable, shall be displayed separately on the website.

Certain remote or restricted areas may not be serviceable. Fioren reserves the right to refuse shipment to locations deemed operationally high-risk or non-serviceable.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, shall be displayed during checkout before payment confirmation.

Fioren reserves the right to modify shipping charges, introduce free shipping thresholds, or apply handling or convenience fees without prior notice.

Order Tracking

Customers may receive order confirmation notifications, shipment confirmation, and tracking details via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

Tracking updates are dependent on courier partner systems and may occasionally experience delays.

Address Accuracy Responsibility

Customers are solely responsible for providing accurate shipping addresses, correct phone numbers, landmark details, and PIN codes.

Fioren shall not be responsible for:

  • Failed deliveries due to incorrect information
  • Delivery delays caused by customer inaccessibility
  • Re-shipping costs arising from incorrect address submission

Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to customer unavailability, incorrect address, repeated non-response, or refusal to accept delivery, the order may:

  • Be returned to origin (RTO)
  • Become ineligible for refund of shipping charges
  • Incur re-shipping fees for redelivery requests

Repeated failed deliveries may result in account restrictions.

Package Inspection Requirement

Customers are advised to inspect packages at the time of delivery. If the outer packaging appears tampered, leaked, damaged, or opened, customers should:

  • Refuse delivery where possible
  • Record a complete unboxing video
  • Contact support immediately

Failure to provide sufficient evidence may result in claim rejection.

Risk of Loss & Title Transfer

Ownership and risk of loss transfer to the customer upon successful delivery confirmation by the courier partner.

Fioren shall not be liable for theft after delivery, loss due to unattended delivery acceptance, or delivery accepted by family members, security personnel, reception desks, or neighbors.

Multiple Shipments

Orders containing multiple products may be shipped separately or delivered in multiple packages, depending on warehouse availability and logistics optimization. No additional shipping fee shall apply unless explicitly stated.

Delays Outside Company Control

Fioren shall not be held liable for delays caused by:

  • Natural calamities
  • Floods
  • Pandemics
  • Government restrictions
  • Customs clearance
  • Transportation strikes
  • Technical failures
  • Logistics disruptions
  • Force majeure events

Delivery commitments shall automatically extend during such circumstances.

International Shipping (If Applicable)

For international orders:

  • Customers are responsible for customs duties, import taxes, and local regulatory compliance
  • Delivery timelines may vary by country
  • Certain products may face import restrictions depending on local laws

Fioren shall not be liable for customs seizures, import restrictions, or regulatory refusals outside India.

Fraud Prevention & Security Checks

To prevent fraudulent transactions, Fioren reserves the right to:

  • Verify customer identity
  • Request additional payment verification
  • Hold suspicious orders
  • Cancel transactions flagged by payment gateways or security systems

Refunds for cancelled prepaid orders due to verification failure shall be processed after internal review.

Limitation of Liability

Fioren’s liability under any shipping or delivery claim shall not exceed the actual amount paid by the customer for the affected order.

Under no circumstances shall Fioren be liable for:
