Step-by-Step

How Do I Apply It?

Step 1
Cleanse
Wash your face with a gentle face cleanser at night.
Step 2
Apply
Apply a pea-sized amount on a clean, toned face and décolletage before sleeping.
Step 3
Massage
Massage gently with fingertips in upward circular motions and allow to absorb fully.
Step 4
Sleep
Leave overnight and let the actives work while you sleep.
Step 5
Protect
Wash your face in the morning and apply a good quality sunscreen before going outdoors.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions.

Product & Usage
Who can use Fioren?
Fioren is suitable for adult men and women across most skin types — normal, dry, oily, combination, and mature skin.
When and how often should I apply Fioren?
Apply every night to clean, dry skin as the final step of your evening routine. Fioren is formulated for daily nighttime use only — it is not advisable during the day as Retinol increases sun sensitivity. In the morning, wash your face and apply a good quality sunscreen.
How long does it take to see visible results?
Many users notice smoother, more hydrated skin within a few weeks of consistent use. For visible improvement in fine lines and skin tone, 4–6 weeks of nightly use is recommended.
Is Fioren suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes, but we recommend performing a patch test before first use. Apply a small amount to the inner forearm and wait 24 hours to check for any reaction.
Is Fioren greasy or sticky?
No. Fioren features a lightweight, non-sticky formula that absorbs quickly into the skin — no heavy residue, just fast-acting nourishment.
Can I apply Fioren on my neck?
Yes. Apply gently to the face, neck, and décolletage as part of your nightly routine for best results.
Is Fioren cruelty-free?
Fioren is developed with a commitment to quality and responsible skincare practices.
